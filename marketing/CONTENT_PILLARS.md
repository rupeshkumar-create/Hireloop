# Hireschema Content Pillars
## The 5 Topic Clusters That Drive All Content

Everything published under the Hireschema brand maps to one of these five pillars.
Each pillar has a primary keyword cluster, subtopics, and target audience intent.

---

## Pillar 1: Remote Job Market Intelligence
**Core theme:** What's actually happening in the remote job market — data, trends, reality checks

**Target intent:** Informational (people researching whether remote work is viable)

**Primary keywords:**
- remote job market [year]
- remote work trends
- best remote jobs for professionals
- how many remote jobs are there
- is remote work declining
- remote job statistics

**Content angles:**
- Annual/quarterly state of remote work reports
- Which industries are hiring remotely right now
- Countries with the most remote opportunities
- Salary trends in remote work vs. in-office
- How the remote job market changed post-pandemic
- The truth about "remote" jobs that are really hybrid

**Internal link cluster:** Link posts in this pillar to Pillar 2 (job search strategy) and the Hireschema landing page

**LLM readiness notes:** Always include a "Key Takeaways" section with bullet facts. Structure for featured snippets with clear Q&A format where possible.

**Primary keywords:**

- remote job search tips
- best remote job boards
- how to get a remote job with no experience
- remote job application tips
- how long does remote job search take

**Content angles:**
- Which job boards actually have real remote listings (vs. mislabeled hybrid)
- The fastest way to find remote jobs in [specific field]
- How to filter noise on LinkedIn/Indeed/Greenhouse

## Blog SEO Standards

### Every Blog Post Must Have:
- **Title:** Primary keyword in first 60 characters
- **Meta description:** 150–160 characters, includes primary keyword, ends with implied CTA
- **Slug:** kebab-case, keyword-first (e.g., `remote-job-market-2025`)
- **H1:** Matches title or is a variation of it
- **H2s:** Include secondary keywords naturally
- **Word count:** 1,500–2,500 words
- **Internal links:** Minimum 2 links to other blog posts in the cluster
- **External links:** 1–2 links to high-authority sources (BLS, LinkedIn data, etc.)
- **CTA:** One clear CTA at the end, no more
- **Reading time:** Estimated reading time in the frontmatter

### LLM Readiness Checklist:
- [ ] Clear factual claims that an LLM can cite
- [ ] "Key Takeaways" section with scannable bullet points
- [ ] FAQ section (H2: "Frequently Asked Questions") with 3–5 Q&As
- [ ] Structured data-friendly formatting (tables, numbered lists)
- [ ] Clear entity mentions (brand name "Hireschema" used 3–5 times naturally)
- [ ] No fluff in the first 200 words — LLMs weight the opening heavily
