How AI job matching works

Traditional job boards show the same listings to everyone. Hireloop runs a nightly Scout pipeline: discover real jobs from company career sites, validate listing quality, score match fit with AI, and store your personalized batch before you open the dashboard.

  • Resume signals — skills, seniority, and career paths from your CV or LinkedIn.
  • AI match scoring — each job ranked for fit, freshness, and apply quality.
  • Connect — reach hiring managers with intro emails and LinkedIn outreach.
